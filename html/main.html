Favorite wines

Jacob's Creek

Jacob's Creek Red is an Australian red wine with intense aromas of red fruits such as cherries and plums, complemented by subtle notes of vanilla. With a medium body and a velvety texture, this wine offers a pleasant and balanced taste, making it ideal to be enjoyed with red meat or pasta.

Serengeti - Red

Serengeti Sweet Red is a sweet red wine with rich aromas of red fruits such as strawberries and raspberries. With a pleasant sweetness and balanced acidity, this wine is ideal to be enjoyed alone or with desserts and light cheeses, offering an accessible and enjoyable tasting experience.

Oliver - Gold

Oliver Sweet White is a sweet white wine, known for its delicious aromas of tropical fruits such as peaches and pineapple. With a delicate sweetness and well-balanced acidity, this wine is perfect to be enjoyed with appetizers, salads or desserts, offering a refined and fresh experience.
